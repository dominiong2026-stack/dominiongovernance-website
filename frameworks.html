Dominion Governance Frameworks

Dominion frameworks organise AI exposure, governance maturity, enterprise value protection and the decision-to-evidence chain. They help leaders move from scattered AI activity to accountable, inspectable governance.

AI Exposure Map

Identifies where AI tools, embedded features, vendors, users, data inputs and decision support are already present.

View framework

Decision-to-Evidence Chain

Connects decisions, policies, approvals, controls, logs, records and board-ready reporting.

Governance Maturity View

Assesses whether AI oversight is ad hoc, documented, controlled, reviewed or embedded into management cadence.

Market-Access Readiness Lens

Tests whether AI governance can withstand client, procurement, investor, insurer, board or regulator scrutiny.

These frameworks support advisory diagnosis and evidence preparation. They do not replace legal advice, statutory audit, cyber assurance or software implementation due diligence.
