Professional Boundaries

Dominion Governance provides AI governance, risk and enterprise-readiness advisory. The work is designed to clarify exposure, strengthen controls, prepare evidence and support accountable implementation.

What Dominion does

Advisory diagnosis, governance design, policy support, control mapping, readiness reviews, board briefings, evidence packs and implementation roadmaps.

What Dominion does not do

Dominion is not a law firm, statutory audit firm, cyber assurance provider, software reseller, regulator or certification body.

Engagement boundaries

  • Formal scope is agreed in writing before advisory work begins.
  • Legal, tax, audit, cyber or regulatory opinions should be obtained from appropriately qualified providers.
  • Recommendations are based on information available during the engagement.
  • Unresolved assumptions and limitations should be recorded rather than hidden.
