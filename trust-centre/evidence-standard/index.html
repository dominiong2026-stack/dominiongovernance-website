Evidence Standard

Dominion Governance treats evidence as a core advisory output. Governance claims should be supported by records, ownership, controls, decisions and review cadence that can be explained under scrutiny.

Evidence before assertion

We avoid unsupported claims of maturity. Findings are framed around available records, observed gaps, documented decisions and practical next steps.

Board-ready documentation

Outputs are designed to help leaders brief boards, management teams, clients and counterparties with clear evidence rather than informal assurances.

Evidence may include

  • AI use inventories and exposure maps.
  • Risk registers, control maps and ownership tables.
  • Policy, acceptable-use and escalation records.
  • Board summaries, action trackers and evidence packs.
  • Documented assumptions, exclusions and unresolved gaps.

Dominion does not issue statutory audit opinions, legal opinions, cyber certifications or regulatory approvals.
