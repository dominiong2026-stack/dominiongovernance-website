Confidentiality and Document Handling

Initial website enquiries should provide enough context for triage without uploading or disclosing highly confidential records. Detailed document review should occur only after scope, confidentiality and handling expectations are agreed.

Initial enquiry standard

Share the business issue, role, market, concern and preferred next step. Avoid sending sensitive client files, employee records, credentials, contracts or proprietary datasets at intake stage.

Engagement-stage handling

Where documents are needed, the engagement should define scope, access, retention expectations, permitted reviewers, confidentiality treatment and evidence outputs.

Practical safeguards

  • Do not submit passwords, system credentials or production datasets through the public form.
  • Redact unnecessary personal or confidential information before sharing examples.
  • Use advisory@dominiongovernance.com for controlled follow-up where appropriate.
  • Agree document handling requirements before any substantive evidence review.
