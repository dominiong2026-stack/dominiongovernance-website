AI Exposure Map

The AI Exposure Map helps leaders identify where AI use already exists, what information may be exposed, which decisions may be influenced, and where accountability or evidence is weak.

Tool and workflow inventory

Maps approved tools, shadow AI, embedded platform features, vendor systems and employee workarounds.

Data exposure view

Assesses whether client, employee, financial, legal, operational or confidential information may enter AI systems.

Decision influence

Identifies where AI outputs may shape advice, approvals, assessments, summaries, analysis or management actions.

Evidence gaps

Highlights missing records, ownership, restrictions, review steps, exception logs and board-ready reporting.

Typical outputs

  • AI exposure snapshot.
  • Data and confidentiality risk themes.
  • Priority control gaps.
  • Recommended evidence pack items.
  • Next-step roadmap for governance work.
