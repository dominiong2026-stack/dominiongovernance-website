About

AI governance, risk and enterprise-readiness advisory.

Dominion Governance helps boards, executives and management teams govern AI, protect enterprise value, strengthen controls, document evidence and prepare for scrutiny.

Dominion is built around disciplined advisory work: identifying unmanaged AI and data exposure, clarifying accountability, strengthening controls, producing usable documentation and helping organisations move from scattered experimentation to governed intelligence.

The firm does not position itself as a software reseller, generic AI agency, law firm, audit firm or cybersecurity assurance provider. Dominion focuses on governance, risk, readiness, evidence and implementation discipline.
