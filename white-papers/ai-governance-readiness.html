AI Governance Readiness for Boards and CEOs

An executive paper on AI readiness, governance maturity, control gaps, and disciplined adoption before AI scales across the organisation.

Executive takeaways

  • Map AI use before expanding automation.
  • Strengthen board and executive oversight.
  • Align AI adoption with measurable business value.

Why this matters

AI adoption can move faster than governance. Before leadership scales AI across workflows, the organisation should know who owns AI decisions, what data and tools are being used, where risk sits, and how value will be measured.

Dominion view

AI governance readiness is not a technology checklist. It is an executive discipline involving accountability, operating control, value measurement, risk visibility, workforce implications, and trust protection.
