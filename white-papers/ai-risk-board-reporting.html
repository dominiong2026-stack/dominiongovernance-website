Risk Reporting

AI Risk & Board Reporting Framework

A structured view of how leadership teams can report AI exposure, staff usage, control weaknesses, policy needs, and board-level risk visibility.

Executive takeaways

  • Build executive risk visibility.
  • Define ownership and escalation.
  • Translate AI risk into board-ready reporting.

Why this matters

AI risk is often hidden in everyday workflow, staff experimentation, external tools, vendor platforms, and unmanaged data handling. Boards and executives need reporting that makes risk visible without turning AI into a technical mystery.

Dominion view

Effective AI risk reporting should connect operational reality to leadership judgement. It should identify what is being used, where exposure exists, who owns the decision, what controls are missing, and what action is required next.
