Controlled AI Adoption Framework

A practical pathway for moving from scattered experimentation to governed, risk-aware AI adoption with human oversight and performance discipline.

Executive takeaways

  • Prioritise high-value use cases.
  • Protect trust and accountability.
  • Scale only when controls and measurement are ready.

Why this matters

Many organisations are already using AI informally before leadership has agreed priorities, controls, value measures, workforce boundaries, or risk tolerance. Controlled adoption turns experimentation into disciplined implementation.

Dominion view

AI should be adopted in stages. Leaders should select use cases with clear business value, assign owners, define controls, maintain human oversight, measure performance, and scale only when governance is ready.
